StyleSpace, by John B. Marine

"StyleSpace, by John B. Marine," or "StyleSpace by JBM" for short, is my blog pertaining to fashion and beauty. I am no expert in fashion, and my blog is not like any other in discussing fashion. Give my fashion/beauty blog a try. You might like the experience! Hopefully, anyways...
